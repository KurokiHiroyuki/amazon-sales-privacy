Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 17, 2025

1. Information We Collect

This application integrates with Amazon's SP-API to access seller data. We collect and process the following information:

  • Personal Identifiable Information (PII): Names, addresses, email addresses, phone numbers of customers and sellers
  • Amazon seller account information: Account details necessary for API authentication
  • Transaction data: Sales data, inventory information, order details, and shipping information through SP-API
  • Usage data: Information related to your interaction with our application

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service functionality
  • Process and analyze your Amazon sales data
  • Improve our application's performance and features
  • Communicate with you about service updates

3. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. All data transmissions are encrypted using industry-standard protocols.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to third parties without your consent, except as described in this policy. We may share data with:

  • Amazon (as required for SP-API functionality)
  • Service providers who assist in our operations (under strict confidentiality agreements)
  • Legal authorities when required by law

5. Data Retention

We retain your personal data only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy, typically for the duration of our service relationship plus any legally required retention period. Transaction data may be retained for up to 7 years for tax and accounting purposes. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us, subject to legal retention requirements.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

7. Amazon SP-API Compliance

This application complies with Amazon's SP-API terms of service, Acceptable Use Policy (AUP), and Data Protection Policy (DPP). We strictly adhere to Amazon's API Developer Agreement and access only the minimum data necessary to provide our services. All data handling practices conform to Amazon's data protection requirements.

8. International Data Transfer

Data processed through Amazon SP-API may be transferred to and stored in countries outside your home country, including the United States where Amazon's servers are located. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and Amazon's global data protection standards.

9. Legal Compliance

This privacy policy complies with applicable data protection laws, including but not limited to Japan's Personal Information Protection Act (PIPA), GDPR where applicable, and other relevant privacy regulations. We are committed to maintaining the highest standards of data protection.
